manifesto

i made this blog to create from the media i consume and cultivate my cultural identity by curating my own taste

my main objectives for this project

  1. no more postponing, either because of the lack of resources, inspiration or motivation; creating this web is free.
  2. give the messy ideas that roam my mind a home, so i can finally have some peace, without relying on simplifyed formats or social media.
  3. learn new skills and learn about new topics by creating media in diverse formarts and investigating
  4. write essays every day in order to practice my writting skills and adress relevant topics in an educated manner
  5. i'd rather collect things online than consume physical media, unless it is strictly necessary, for it contaminates and supports wicked industries

things i will activly avoid

  • utilizing any form of AI
  • stealing intelectual property or copying others
  • encapsulating myself, whether it is aesthetics or mindsets
  • giving place to negative thoughts
  • generalisation and falasies
