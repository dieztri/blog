manifesto

i made this blog to create from the media i consume and cultivate my cultural identity by curating my own taste.

my main objectives for this project

  1. KILL PROCASTINATION ~ no more postponing, either because of the lack of resources, inspiration or motivation; creating this web is free.
  2. CLEAN MY BRAIN ~ give the messy ideas that roam my mind a home, so i can finally have some peace, without relying on simplifyed formats or social media.
  3. FIGHT BRAINROT ~ learn new skills and learn about new topics by creating media in diverse formarts and investigating
  4. BIRTH INSIGHTS ~ write essays every day in order to practice my writting skills and adress relevant topics in an educated manner
  5. AVOID CONSUMISM ~ i'd rather collect things online than consume physical media, unless it is strictly necessary, for it contaminates and supports wicked industries

things i will activly avoid

  • utilizing any form of AI
  • stealing intelectual property or copying others
  • encapsulating myself, whether it is aesthetics, genres or mindsets
  • giving place to negative thoughts
  • generalization and falasies
